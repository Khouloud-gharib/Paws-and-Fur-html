Welcome to "Paws and Fur"

At Paws and Fur, we believe every cat and dog deserves a loving home. Our mission is to connect abandoned, rescued, and homeless pets with caring individuals and families, creating lifelong bonds that bring joy and companionship.

We work closely with local shelters, foster homes, and animal rescue organizations to ensure that every animal in our care is healthy, happy, and ready to find their forever home. Whether you're looking for a playful kitten, a loyal dog, or a gentle senior pet, we provide personalized guidance to help you find the perfect match.

Adopting a pet is more than just bringing a new friend into your life; it’s a chance to make a positive impact on a life that needs love. Join us in making a difference. Together, we can give every pet the second chance they deserve.

Start your journey with us today, and meet the pet who could be your new best friend!

Why Adopt a Pet?

Adopting a pet is a life-changing decision. When you choose to adopt, you're not only gaining a loyal companion but also giving an animal a second chance at a happy life. Whether you're looking for a playful puppy, a calm and affectionate senior cat, or anything in between, your perfect match is waiting for you!

Adopting from us means:
  • Saving a life and reducing overpopulation.
  • Gaining a pet that is spayed/neutered, vaccinated, and health-checked.
  • Supporting a community that cares about animal welfare.

Our Adoption Process

We believe that every animal deserves a loving home, and we're here to make the adoption process smooth and stress-free. Here's how it works:

1. Browse Our Pets
Explore the profiles of our available dogs, cats, and other animals. Each pet has a unique story and personality, and we're here to help you find the perfect match.
2. Meet Your Match
Once you’ve found a pet you’re interested in, schedule a meet-and-greet! Whether it's online or in person, this step ensures you and your future pet get to know each other.
3. Apply for Adoption
After the meet-and-greet, if you feel this is the right pet for you, complete our easy online adoption application. We'll review your information and be in touch to guide you through the next steps.
4. Home Sweet Home
Once approved, we’ll arrange for your new pet to go home with you. We'll provide resources and tips to help make the transition smooth and ensure your pet feels right at home.

Our Commitment to You

We’re dedicated to ensuring successful adoptions. That’s why we offer post-adoption support, including guidance on training, behavior, and health care. Our team is always here to answer your questions and provide assistance.
